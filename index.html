Questions Posed By Virginia Voting Trends

Does frequency of voting change with income and education levels?

This plot shows the correlation between voting category, income, and education. In a loose trend, more frequent voting tends to correlate with higher education and income levels. On the other side, less frequent voting tends to correlate with lower levels of education and income levels. While this loose trend does exist, voters from every category appear in every education and income level.




How did voter turnout and absentee voting change in Virginia General Elections from 2014 to 2022?

A dramatic spike in absentee voting is seen in 2020 because of the Covid-19 pandemic and the percentage is still higher than in previous years in 2022. An increase in voter turnout is seen in presidential election years including 2016 and 2020.




What did voter turnout look like in cities and counties in Virginia in the 2022 general election?

The percentage voter turnout in Virginia in 2022 varied by county between 33% and 67%. There were to be higher levels of voter turnout in Northern Virginia and along the eastern coast than in parts of Southern and Western Virginia.
